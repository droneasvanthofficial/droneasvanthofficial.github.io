About Me

Hello, I'm Asvanth, a dedicated drone pilot with a strong passion for aerial technology. My interest in drones began at a young age, inspired by a fascination with flight. This passion was nurtured when my parents gifted me my first drone, SkyRider, which I quickly learned to pilot with precision. I have honed my skills through various projects, including capturing high-quality aerial photography and participating in drone racing competitions, where I won first place and earned a scholarship to a renowned drone piloting academy. My expertise extends to environmental conservation efforts and search and rescue missions, where I utilize drone technology for practical applications. I am eager to bring my skills and enthusiasm for drone technology to your team, contributing to innovative projects and advancing the field of aerial robotics
